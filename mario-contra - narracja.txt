**Mario-Contra** — a wild fusion, a daring blend of three worlds: Contra, Mario Bros, and Ghosts 'n Goblins (or Ghouls, if you will).  
Three realms to explore, numbered in the Mario style, each echoing its own tale.  
And yes, you can rack up points along the way.

So, let's begin...

**Run through the haunted graveyard!**  
**Gather shining coins!**  
**Face grotesque monsters from the depths of horror!**  

Kill the princess, free the dragon, or something grand like that...
But beware — don’t accidentally strike down the one you seek to save!

And when the quest is done...

**Go to the skies in a helicopter, soaring into the abyss!**

**Violence, sex, corpses, dragons, and princesses!**  
Why bother with three games separately, when you can live them all in one?

**MARIO-CONTRA!!!**

Note: The game's creator advises against shooting graves in real life!
