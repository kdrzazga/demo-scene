"Electrician" — a remake of my beloved game for small Atari from the days of my youth.

Of course, as a Commodore kid, I played it quietly, sneaking at my cousin’s place.
No chance I’d admit to it at school or on the playground...
The original was crafted by the late David Bunch in 1984.
To this day, as far as I know, no ports have appeared elsewhere... until now!

The game features five levels and is simpler than the original,
but just like its predecessor, it’s all about... pulling the wire.

Enter the darkened buildings!
Fight your way thru hordes of rodents and spiders, bring light to the dwellers!
AMEN!

Flee from giant spiders, fry the rats with electric charge, dodge the bats!
Electricity, my friend, does not discriminate!!!

Kick Atari fans ... well, never mind!

230 volts for everyone!
Pull the wire with the Electrician!
